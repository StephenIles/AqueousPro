Driveways, Paths, Patios

There are many different types of driveways such as tarmac, block paving, resins and patterned concrete that require different techniques to clean and maintain them all year round.

This includes our pressurised steam cleaning equipment that not only cleans it also kills growth due to the heat, which means in most cases we do not need to use any chemicals, saving both money and the environment.

Unfortunately, this solution is not suitable for all driveways, but please feel free to get in contact to discuss what you have and the available options.
