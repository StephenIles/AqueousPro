About us

Welcome to our family-run exterior cleaning business, proudly based in West Berkshire and serving the local area

While we're a family team at heart, we take our work seriously, offering professional and high-quality services to both residential and commercial clients.

From window cleaning and gutter clearing to pressure washing, we’re here to meet all your exterior cleaning needs.

Our vehicles are clearly marked, and we've invested in top-of-the-line equipment that's regularly maintained and fully up-to-date with all necessary inspections. Our friendly team is always dressed in Aqueous Pro uniforms and each member is DBS checked to ensure both your safety and ours.

We’re also proud to say that our team is trained by the British Window Cleaning Academy in advanced Water Fed Pole (WFP) techniques. We have specialists trained in roof cleaning and soft wash, pressure, and steam cleaning to ensure the best results for every job.

Your peace of mind is important to us, which is why we carry comprehensive insurance, including public liability coverage of £5 million, along with additional protection for your home and property in the unlikely event of an incident.

We offer a wide range of services, including but not limited to:

  • Driveways, patios and decking
  • Roofs, Moss and Biological Growth Removal
  • Solar panels and conservatories cleaning
  • Gutters soffits and facia cleraning and repairs
  • Windows, frames and sills

If staining or deep set organic growth needs to be removed from a surface, we can offer advice on various treatments to deal with the problem.

Whether you're looking for a one-off clean or a regular maintenance schedule, we’re happy to tailor our services to suit your needs. From occasional treatments to monthly, quarterly, or yearly cleanings, or even a bespoke maintenance package – we’ve got you covered!
