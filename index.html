About us

We are a family-run External Cleaning business based in West Berkshire covering the local area.

Although we are a family business, we are also very professional and work to a very high standard. We pride ourselves in providing a comprehensive range of services for Residential and Commercial clients ranging from window cleaning to gutter cleaning and pressure washing.

All our working vehicles are sign written, we have invested in the best equipment which is serviced and up to date with all inspections, all our staff are well presented in an Aqueous Pro uniform and they are all DBS checked for your and our safety.

They have also been BWCA (British Window Cleaning Academy) trained in WFP (Water Fed Pole) window cleaning technology and we have specialist staff trained in roof cleaning and soft wash/pressure/steam cleaning.
