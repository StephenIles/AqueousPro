Our Services

Pressure Washing

We have very powerful petrol pressure washers with all-associated equipment including lances, both turbo and standard nozzles and large surface cleaners. All this equipment allows us to tackle a lot of your dirt and grime jobs with pressure up to 3000 psi.

Steam

As well as traditional high powered pressure washers, we have also added a “hot box” system to our equipment portfolio, which means we can produce steam up to 140°C at 2500 psi if required.

This allows us to deliver steam under controlled high pressure, which in most cases, will clear all the dirt, grime and any growth, thus eliminating the requirement of any type of chemical treatment. It also has less water runoff due to the evaporation. This may not clear all stubborn dirt and growth but will remove most of it. Not all surfaces are suitable for a steam clean, but one of our technicians will be able to discuss the available options with you.

Please ask us about our re-sanding service for block driveways.

Pure Water Cleaning for Windows, Solar Panels, Conservatories and Glass Roofs

We invested in Reverse Osmosis Pure Water Systems which are installed in our vehicles. Pure water technology allows us to clean your windows, solar panels, frames, soffits, facias and conservatories without using any harmful chemicals. The pure water produced, “clings” onto the dirt and washes it away. The addition of a hot water system allows us to remove stubborn dirt that maybe present on your glass and frames.

Step 1: Windows, frames and sills are lightly scrubbed with a soft-headed brush.

Step 2: Windows are rinsed with Pure Water (fed through the extendable poles), and left to dry.

Step 3: Enjoy sparkling windows all year round, with a high presentation finish for everyone to see.

Because the water we use has had all its impurities removed, the windows will dry naturally, leaving no smears or spots - just a pure shine every time. What’s more, they’ll stay cleaner for longer.

This is delivered via WFP (water fed poles) by our technicians from the ground, if required we can go up to 7 floors using this type of technology. This eliminates the use of ladders which could potentially ruin lawns and any associated health and safety risks associated with their use.

Gutter Clearing and Cleaning

If blocked gutters are left too long, it can cause serious water damage to your property, which in turn encourages mould, growth and damp.

Our trained staff can clear your gutters and get them back functioning properly, leading the water away from your property and ultimately protecting your investment. We can clean gutters up to 4 storeys high from the ground via our high-powered vacuum and pole system.

Our services include a cosmetic exterior clean, as well as a functional interior clean, which removes all unwanted debris from gutters and down pipes.
